Some experiments never end.

Seven Days After is a descent into grief, obsession, and something far worse than death. After a revolutionary cancer treatment turns into a catastrophic massacre inside the Groves Research Facility, survivors Markus Oakley, Jay Togay, and Flake Groves are pulled into a nightmare hidden beneath the city.


As failed experiments roam abandoned corridors and the facility begins changing itself from the inside out, the line between human and monster starts to disappear. Haunted by guilt and hunted by the very system he created, Flake must confront the terrifying truth behind PROJECT UNKNOWN before it spreads beyond containment.


Blending psychological horror, science fiction, survival, and emotional collapse, Seven Days After explores what happens when the desire to save humanity becomes the thing that destroys it.
